Handling of Copyright Requests​

  • Upon receiving a valid content removal request, the specific lyrics and/or melodies will be removed from the Service.
  • Transliterations and translations contributed by users may still be argued under fair use. However, to avoid potential legal conflicts, they will also be made inaccessible and non-editable if a removal request is received.
  • Contributors will no longer be able to add, view, or edit the lyrics, transliterations, translations, and melodies for the removed content.
  • The song itself will not be removed from the Service, as it is hosted externally (e.g., YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, SoundCloud). To remove the song entirely, the copyright owner needs to approach the hosting platform directly.
  • Previous edits containing the infringing lyrics and/or melodies will be disabled and made inaccessible to users.
  • A transparency notice will be added to the edit history indicating that certain edits have been disabled due to copyright claims.
