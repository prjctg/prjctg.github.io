Privacy Policy

1. Introduction​

Welcome to Project G. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect​

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Google OAuth, we collect your unique ID and email address to identify your account.
  • Username: You choose a unique username for your account.
  • User Contributions: We collect content you contribute, such as song lyrics and animation code.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to collect aggregate data about your usage of our website, such as pages visited and time spent on the site. Google Analytics uses cookies only if you give your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information​

  • To Manage Your Account: We use your unique ID and email address to manage your account.
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze usage data from Google Analytics to enhance your experience on our website.
  • To Display Content: We display your contributions (e.g., song lyrics, animation code) on our platform for other users to view and interact with.

4. Embedded Content from External Sites​

Our platform may display embedded videos and audio from sites like YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, and SoundCloud. These external sites may collect data about you according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.

5. Third-Party Hosting and Scripts​

  • Hosting: Our site is hosted on vercel.app, and our documentation and other resources are hosted on github.io. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.
  • Scripts: We use scripts from content delivery networks (CDNs) like jsdelivr.com and esm.sh. These third parties may collect data according to their privacy policies.
  • Images: Users can link to images hosted on cloudinary.com. Cloudinary may collect data according to their privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing​

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. We use Google Analytics to collect aggregate data about site visitors to help us improve our services. Google Analytics does not receive any personal information from us.

7. Data Retention​

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. User-contributed content remains on the platform even if your account is deleted to preserve collaborative contributions.

8. Data Security​

  • We encrypt your personal information, like email addresses, when storing it.
  • Your personal information is also protected with encryption when sent over the internet.
  • Only the project owner can access your personal information.

9. Use of Microphone, Camera, and Clipboard​

  • Microphone and Camera: Some interactive features may request access to your microphone or camera. You will always be prompted for permission before access is granted. Any data captured by these devices is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted or stored by us. We ensure this by:

    • Running all user-contributed code in a secure sandboxed environment.
    • Enforcing a strict Content Security Policy (CSP) that only allows connections to approved domains and CDNs, preventing unauthorized data transmission.

  • Clipboard: Our platform may access your clipboard content to facilitate features like copy-pasting song lyrics. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored by us.

10. Peer-to-Peer Connections​

  • WebRTC Usage: Some features of the application may use WebRTC for direct communication between users' devices. These peer-to-peer connections are initiated and established by users, and do not transmit audio or video. They are used for real-time interactions, such as data sharing.
    • We use Google's public STUN servers to help establish these connections. STUN servers assist in setting up the connection but do not transmit or store your data.
  • Security Measures: We ensure that peer-to-peer connections are secure and comply with our existing security policies.

11. User Rights​

You have rights regarding your personal data, including accessing it, requesting corrections, and withdrawing consent for processing. If you have concerns or questions about your data, contact us at [email address or contact form].

12. Cookies and Local Storage​

  • We use Google Analytics to collect aggregate visitor data with your consent.
  • User preferences and other data, such as favorite songs or cached content, may be stored locally in your browser’s local storage or IndexedDB.
  • For more information on Google Analytics' data collection and processing, visit their privacy policy.

13. Children’s Privacy​

  • Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it.
  • Children aged 13-18 must have parental consent to use our services.

14. International Data Processing​

As our service is accessible worldwide, your data may be stored or processed in countries other than your own. We ensure your data is adequately protected according to applicable data protection laws.

15. Links to Third-Party Sites​

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

16. Data Breach Procedures​

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will take appropriate measures to protect your data and notify you if necessary.

17. Opt-Out Information​

  • You cannot opt out of email address collection as it is essential for account identification.
  • However, you can opt out of data collection for website analytics by disabling cookies in your browser or using a browser plugin.

18. Changes to the Privacy Policy​

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website.

19. Contact Information​

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us using the methods provided on our Feedback Page.

20. Legal Compliance​

We comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, PDPA (Singapore), and other relevant regulations.

21. Data Subject Rights​

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the methods provided on our Feedback Page.
